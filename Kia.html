Foreign Kia Car Models Available for Import to India


Kia Telluride – A large 7-seater SUV, popular in the U.S. and other markets for its spaciousness and premium features.

Kia Stinger – A sports sedan with powerful engine options and a sleek design, popular in Europe and the U.S.

Kia K5 (Optima) – A mid-size sedan with advanced technology and safety features, available in the U.S. and other markets.

Kia Sorento – A mid-size SUV with both hybrid and plug-in hybrid options, highly popular in the U.S.

Kia Carnival (Sedona) – A premium MPV (multi-purpose vehicle), known for its luxury features and comfort in foreign markets.

Kia Cadenza (K7) – A full-size sedan with advanced features, available in markets like South Korea and the U.S.

Kia Soul EV – An electric compact crossover SUV with a distinctive design, available in some international markets.

Kia Forte (Cerato) – A compact sedan known for its affordability and efficiency, available in the U.S. and select international markets.

Kia Seltos (Global) – Though available in India, some international variants of the Seltos have different features and engine options, which can be imported.

Kia Rio – A compact hatchback or sedan known for its practicality and affordability, available in various international markets.
